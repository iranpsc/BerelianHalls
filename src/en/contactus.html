We have a nearby NCP carpark approximately 5 minutes’ walk away.
Due to the building being Grade 1 listed we work closely with a list of approved suppliers who know the venue well, all suppliers must be from our list
We offer standard hours of 0900 access to 0200 which includes a two hour load out for the suppliers. These timings can be extended depending on the event at an additional fee.
