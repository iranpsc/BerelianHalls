THE GALLERIES

SOANE HALL

Experience the grandeur of One Marylebone's double height Galleries on the first floor. The 'U' shaped space is split into East, West, and South Galleries, with natural daylight flooding the former two through floor-to-ceiling windows. Perfect for hosting your next big event, the Galleries can be hired as one space or as a breath-taking reception area for a main function in the Soane Hall.
